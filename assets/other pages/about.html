Our Journey

Principal Muhammad Ali

Meet our esteemed Principal, a distinguished individual with a Ph.D. earned from NED University.

Over the last 15 years, we began our journey just like the emerging trends of meggings, fingerstache, and post-irony in Portland. Gradually, we grew, faced challenges, and learned along the way. With each small success, we celebrated and dared to think differently. As we move forward, we fondly cherish our beginnings and remain determined to build a bright future, one step at a time. Our path to success was like savoring a special flavor - a blend of craftsmanship and innovation. We nurtured close connections, celebrated each milestone, and explored new opportunities. Our journey may have been asymmetrical, but it led us to unique and meaningful experiences that we proudly share with the world.
